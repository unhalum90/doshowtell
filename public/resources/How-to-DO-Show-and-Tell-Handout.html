How to DO Show and Tell

Use this guide to help you explain your thinking clearly. Remember: the goal isn't just to get the right answer, but to share how you got there.

1. DO

Do the work first. Before you record your voice, make sure you have it in front of you.

  • Annotate the text or image.
  • Solve the math problem on paper.
  • Draw a diagram or map.
  • Highlight key evidence.

2. SHOW

Look at your work while you talk. Use your visuals to guide your explanation.

  • Look specific words or numbers as you mention them.
  • Trace the path or logic on your diagram with your finger.
  • Use your notes to keep your explanation focused.

3. TELL

Explain your thinking. Connect the dots for your audience.

  • "I started by..."
  • "I noticed that..."
  • "This part was tricky because..."
  • "I know this is true because..."
