## 2. Sentence Stems for Voice Recording

**Format:** PDF  
**Grades:** All grades  
**Tagline:** Scaffolding prompts to help students structure their explanations

### Title
**Sentence Stems for Voice Responses: Say What You Know**

### Instructions (for students)
When you're answering questions about your work, these sentence starters can help you explain your thinking clearly. You don't have to use them allâ€”pick the ones that fit the question!

### Materials Included

**When Asked "What did you do?"**
- "First, I..."
- "I started by..."
- "My approach was to..."

**When Asked "Why did you do it that way?"**
- "I chose to _____ because..."
- "I thought this would work because..."
- "I remembered that _____, so I..."
- "This made sense to me because..."

**When Asked About a Specific Part of Your Work:**
- "That part shows..."
- "I did that because..."
- "I was thinking about..."

**When Asked to Connect to What You've Learned:**
- "This reminds me of..."
- "I used what I learned about _____ to..."
- "This is similar to _____ because..."

**When Asked About Challenges or Changes:**
- "The tricky part was..."
- "I got stuck when _____, so I..."
- "I changed my approach because..."
- "If I did this again, I would..."

**When Asked What You Learned:**
- "Now I understand that..."
- "The most important thing is..."
- "This taught me..."
